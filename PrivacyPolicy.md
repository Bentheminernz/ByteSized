# Privacy Policy  
**ByteSized**  
Effective Date: March 3, 2026  

## Overview

ByteSized respects your privacy.

ByteSized is designed to operate entirely on your device.  
The app does not connect to the internet, does not use external servers, and does not transmit any data.

Simply put:

> ByteSized collects no data — period.

---

## Information Collection

ByteSized does **not** collect, store, process, or share any of the following:

- Personal information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics
- AI prompts or inputs
- Cookies or tracking data
- Crash reports
- Diagnostic data

The app does not require user accounts or sign-ins.

---

## Internet Connectivity

ByteSized does not connect to the internet.

All features function entirely offline.  
No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

---

## On-Device Data

Any content, preferences, or learning progress remains stored locally on your device.

ByteSized developers do not have access to this information.

---

## Third-Party Services

ByteSized does not use:

- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Cloud storage providers
- External APIs
- Tracking technologies of any kind

---

## Data Sharing

Because ByteSized does not collect data, no data is shared, sold, rented, or disclosed to any third parties.

---

## Children's Privacy

ByteSized does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If ByteSized ever introduces features that require internet connectivity or data collection, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those features are released.

The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.
